Personal information refers to any details that can be used to identify you, such as:

Non-personal information does not directly identify you and may include:

  • To operate and maintain the Website: Ensuring the proper functionality and security of the Website.
  • To improve user experience: Analyzing how users interact with the Website to enhance its design and performance.

  • To send promotional communications: With your consent, we may send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials related to our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations: We may be required to collect and process your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:
